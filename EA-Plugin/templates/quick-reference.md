# Uppit AI Plugin — Quick Reference

Pin this somewhere you'll see it until the habit sticks.

---

## Daily (30 seconds)

Tell your agent what you worked on. That's it.

> "Finished the email sequence. Landing page is halfway done. Blocked on getting copy feedback from Jon."

Your agent updates ClickUp automatically.

**Command:** `/uppit:update-tasks` or just type your update naturally.

---

## Before a Meeting with Jon

Queue up anything you want to discuss:

> `/uppit:discussion-items Jon Need to talk about the Q2 timeline for the course launch`

Your talking points will show up in Jon's daily briefing before the meeting.

---

## Anytime

Ask your agent about your tasks:
- "What's on my plate this week?"
- "What's overdue?"
- "What am I waiting on from others?"

---

## That's it.

No ClickUp UI. No forgetting to update statuses. Just talk to your agent.
